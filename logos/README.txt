Drop a PNG for each team here. The filename must match the team name exactly
as it appears in data.csv, with spaces replaced by underscores, plus .png.

Examples:
  Charlotte Hornets       -> Charlotte_Hornets.png
  Golden State Warriors   -> Golden_State_Warriors.png
  Los Angeles Lakers      -> Los_Angeles_Lakers.png
  Oklahoma City Thunder   -> Oklahoma_City_Thunder.png
  Portland Trail Blazers  -> Portland_Trail_Blazers.png

Aspect ratio doesn't matter; the UI fits each logo into a 140x90 cell while
preserving aspect. Any team that doesn't have a logo here will render as a
colored rectangle with the team name overlaid (so the app still works without
all 30 PNGs in place).

NBA team logos are copyrighted; source your own files (e.g. from the team's
official press kit or your school's licensed assets). Don't commit them to
public repos.
